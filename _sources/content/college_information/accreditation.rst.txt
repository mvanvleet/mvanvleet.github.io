Statement of Accreditation
==========================

Spelman College is accredited by the Commission of Colleges of the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools (SACSCOC) to award Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Science degrees. In addition, the following programs are accredited by discipline-specific agencies:
* National Association for College Admissions Counseling
* National Association of Schools of Music
* Georgia Professional Standards Commission (Teacher Education)
* American Chemical Society
